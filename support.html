üì± How do I restore my purchases?

If you've purchased a subscription or lifetime unlock and need to restore it:

  1. Open AnimalFun
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll down to "Subscription Management"
  4. Tap "Restore Purchases"
  5. Your previous purchase will be restored automatically

Note: You must be using the same Apple ID that was used for the original purchase.

üîÑ How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. To cancel:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Find "AnimalFun" in the list
  5. Tap it and select "Cancel Subscription"

You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

üì∏ How does the photo collection feature work?

Kids can personalize their experience by adding their own animal photos:

  1. View any animal in the app
  2. Tap the camera icon
  3. Take a new photo or select from your photo library
  4. Your photo will replace the stock image in your personal view

This creates a personal connection and makes learning more engaging!

üîä The animal sounds aren't playing. What should I do?

If animal sounds aren't working:

  • Check that your device isn't on silent mode
  • Ensure the volume is turned up
  • Go to AnimalFun Settings and verify sounds are enabled
  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • Restart your device if the issue persists
